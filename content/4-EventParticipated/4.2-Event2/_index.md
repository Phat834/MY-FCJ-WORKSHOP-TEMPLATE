# Tọa đàm Thu hoạch: Tech Startup Orientation & Driving Innovation with AI

### Event Objectives
* Gain practical insights from founders and CEOs regarding technology entrepreneurship in the Generative AI era.
* Understand the systematic workflow of transforming a raw tech idea into a Minimum Viable Product (MVP).
* Identify essential skills and career roadmaps for IT students navigating the wave of automation.

### Key Seminar Highlights

#### 1. Executive Perspectives on the Current AI Market Landscape
* AI is no longer a temporary trend but has evolved into a foundational infrastructure for modern enterprise applications.
* A prominent market shift from pure research on large AI models toward "Applied AI" – leveraging AI to solve specific user pain points.

#### 2. Practical Lessons in Building Tech Products
* **Avoiding the Technology Trap:** Software engineers frequently over-focus on making algorithms complex while overlooking whether the product remains user-friendly or aligns with actual market demands.
* **Cost Optimization from Day One:** When running AI-driven applications, API invocation and computing infrastructure costs can escalate rapidly. Therefore, designing optimized system architectures from the start is vital for any product's survival.

#### 3. Career Advice for Computer Science & IT Students
* Adaptability outweighs static, rigid knowledge. Technologies evolve monthly; hence, the capacity for continuous self-directed learning is the ultimate key to success.
* Modern software engineers need to equip themselves with strong communication skills and a product-oriented mindset rather than focusing solely on writing raw code.

### Personal Reflections & Takeaways
This open-forum fireside chat alleviated much of the pressure regarding rapid technological shifts. Instead of fearing AI replacement, I realized that by mastering AI tools and cultivating a robust product mindset, AI will serve as a powerful catalyst to enhance my productivity and accelerate my software engineering career.
